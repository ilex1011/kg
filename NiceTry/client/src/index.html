About KG(HK)
關於KG

「KG」是行業內的物流專家，代理了許多貨運服務， 配合完善的海外國際代理網絡，能提供穩定及定期的集散及裝箱國際貨運服務，以極吸引而又合理的價格提供優質的服務予客戶。

憑著完善的海外國際代理網絡，我們的服務範圍遍佈世界各地的國際機場，港口；以便客戶可以追蹤貨物的運送情況及到達目的地的日期及時間。 通過此網絡，讓供應商或客戶，可以更加有效地掌握訂貨，運送的資料，從而節省時間及金錢。

KG(HK) is one of the leading master consolidators in the industry with many airlines’ airwaybill stocks. With blocked space contracts/agreements of different airlines and in conjunction with our network of global agents throughout the world, we are able to build up reliable and regular consolidations via major airlines servicing international routes, thus, we are in the position to offer attractive and competitive rates to our valuable clients.

Our International Agency Network covers all major international ports/airports. Our network allows us to track your orders from your suppliers to your destined countries. Through this network, we have the ability to consolidate orders and assist in the sourcing of new suppliers thus saving our clients time and money. We have our own offices located in Bangkok, Los Angeles, New York, Tokyo, Singapore, Kuala Lumpur, Shanghai, Tianjin, Guangzhou, Xiamen and Shenzhen and have more than 200 reliable and efficient associate agents worldwide.
